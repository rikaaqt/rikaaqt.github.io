	♡ HOW TO USE/INSTALL ON YOUR HOME COMPUTER (WINDOWS) ♡

To switch cursor schemes on windows, go to Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Mouse > Additional Mouse Settings and navigate to the Pointers tab. On the Pointers tab, click Browse, then Save As... to name as a new scheme.
